Chocolate Brownies

Indulge in these rich and fudgy chocolate brownies that are perfect for any sweet tooth!

Whether you eat them warm from the oven with ice cream or pack them as a snack, these brownies hit different every single time. A must-try for any chocolate lover.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup milk

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then add the vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased 8x8 inch baking pan.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Cool completely before cutting into squares.
